    6 signs your website needs a redesign

    Your website is your most powerful digital marketing tool. When it is no longer perfoming, then it's time you start thinking about redesigning your website. This can be a hard decision for businesses to know when it's the time for a website redesign.

    So, we have put together a list of 6 signs to help you know if it's time to redesign your website.

    1.  Your website is difficult to navigate and find information

    Your website's navigation structure is a critical element. It should allow your website visitors to find out where they are, where they have been, and direct them where to go. If your website's visitors can't figure out how to find the information they need, then you lose leads on your site.

    How do you know if your site's navigation is easy to use.

    To find out, one way is to test it yourself. You can consider yourself as one of your website's visitor looking for a particular product or information. For example, if you're an ecommerce, you can visit a product page and purchase an item.

    You can also navigate from your website's homepage through the other pages, or you can ask a friend to try navigate your site and ask for feedback on how the feel and experience your site.

    2.  Your current website visitors are not converting to sales.

    Your business website should be designed to drive leads and convert them to sales. Your site visitors should navigate easily through your site, check out your information, products or services, and make purchases. If your website is having difficulty doing that, then you may need a website redesign serice.

    For your website to increase conversion rates, it needs some critical elements, which can range from easy navigation structure, adding effective call to action (CTA) button in your website pages. These elements can get you from losing leads to earning conversions.

    At Crosint, we can help you maximize your conversion rates and improve your ROI.

    3.  Your competitors have improved their websites

    You want your website to retain it's highly competitive edge. If your competitors updates or redesigns their sites and you don't, it's easy for customers to choose them over you.

    Users today often want to interact with a fresh, clean and modern brand. If your website looks old and outdated, you could be at a disadvantage in a head to head matchup with your competitors.

    Visit your competitors' websites today and see if it looks modern, recently updated and if it's easier to navigate, then compare it with your site. If your site is lagging behind, you may already be losing potential customers, and definitely that's a sign that it's time for a website redesign.

    4.  Your site doesn't rank well on search engines

    If your website does not rank well on search engines then it's bad for business. This indicates that your site has a problem and it lacks proper optimization.

    As your website is your core digital marketing tool, it should appear on the first page of search results, otherwise it could be time for a website redesign. It's always recommended to have a search engine optimization (SEO) strategy for your website.

    With SEO, users can easily fin you online. This allows your website to drive in traffic, generate more leads and maximize your conversion rates.

    3 important website redesign practices for success

    Before you dive in to your website redesign process, it's important to formulate an effective strategy that will improve your results. Here are some 3 best practices to get started with:

    1.  Enhance your user experience (UX) design

    One of the most important aspects of website redesign is user experience (UX) design. UX design helps you have a deep understanding of your users, their needs, their values and also their limitations.

    User experience (UX) design enhances your website to provide your users with a great experience, engage them better and improve your conversions. If your website is having difficulties making users comfortable and confident while navigating your site, it's time for a website redesign to improve your user experience (UX) design.

    Crosint offers professional website redesign services that improve the usability of your website, to improve user experience and increase customer satisfaction.

    2.  Add strong visual features and elements

    Many people respond more strongly to visual elements like images and videos, than to plain text. This shows how impactful visual features and elements are to your website.

    When you use images, videos, illustrations and other visual features on your website, you encourage your website's user to keep interested and engaged on your web pages. This also helps to turn users to customers.

    When you work with a professional website redesign company like Crosint, you can get effective visual features and elements in your site that keeps your audience more interested and engaged on your web pages.

    However, it's crucial to ensure that your visual features display well on your mobile website and mobile devices.

    3.  Optimize your entire website for SEO

    One of the most critical aspects for your website is its ability to rank on search engines. That's why search engine optimization (SEO) is critical to your website redesign.

    SEO is the process of optimizing your website pages and content to increase its visibility on search engines. The more your website is visible on relevant search results, the more likely you are to attract valuable prospects to your business.

    If your site is not optimized for SEO, your website's perfomance on search results will be poor. With this you lose traffic and leads, and it's time to redesign your site.
